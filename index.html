How to Read a Stock Chart

Learning how to read a stock chart can help you analyze patterns, make educated guesses about future price movements, and time your entry/exit better. Start by understanding candlesticks, moving averages, and volume trends.

ETF vs Mutual Funds

ETFs are traded like stocks and usually have lower fees, while mutual funds are actively managed and may offer better long-term strategies. Choose based on your investment goals, cost sensitivity, and time horizon.

Top Stocks in 2025

Some of the most promising stocks include those in AI, green energy, and biotech sectors. Look out for companies with strong balance sheets, innovation pipelines, and consistent revenue growth.
